About the Website

A Compilation of His Works Throughout the Semester

Conclusion

Learnings and Reflections

Throughout my experience in this course, in my professor, and in System Administration, in general, I learned a lot of valuable lessons. I never expected that I will enjoy this course, since I kind of chose my elective randomly. Surprisingly, I very much enjoyed the learning process.

As a future System Administrator, it is crucial for me to have a basic knowledge of automation of processes, thanks for Ansible for easing things up; being able to automate processes saves both time and effort which in the industry also saves a lot of money. Additionally, I would like to express my gratitude to our Instructor who is very forgiving with his deadlines and workloads. Overall, this course has been fun, and I am excited and looking forward to our next semester's elective course.
